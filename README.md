# PositionSight AI

PositionSight AI is an AI-powered strategy skill for crypto traders.

It helps users understand their positions visually by combining their entry price, current market data, risk profile, and CoinMarketCap data to generate clear, explainable, and backtestable trading strategies.

## Problem

Most crypto platforms show trading positions mainly as numbers. Traders often struggle to understand where they entered, whether their trade is still valid, and what strategy they should follow next.

## Solution

PositionSight AI displays the user’s entry price, current price, profit/loss zone, stop-loss area, take-profit levels, and AI-generated strategy rules in a visual interface.

## Main Features

* Add a crypto asset and entry price.
* Fetch market data from CoinMarketCap.
* Display entry price and current price visually.
* Generate an AI-assisted trading strategy.
* Define stop loss, take profit, invalidation level, timeframe, and risk logic.
* Export the strategy as a structured specification for backtesting.
